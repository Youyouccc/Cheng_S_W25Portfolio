Capstone Project


Project name: FindCareer

"Find Career" is a web-based recruitment platform that connects job seekers and employers, facilitating a smooth, intuitive, and efficient job search and hiring process. The platform enables job seekers to create verified profiles, apply for jobs, and manage their applications, while employers can post job listings, screen applications, and manage recruitment workflows.


Project Vision

Our vision is to create a seamless job search and recruitment platform that leverages technology to enhance the job-seeking experience for individuals and streamline the hiring process for employers. By implementing advanced features like AI-driven job recommendations and real-time notifications, we aim to ensure that both job seekers and employers have the most efficient and effective platform possible.


Project/Business Requirements

    User Registration and Profile Management:
  • Job seekers can register, create, and verify profiles with relevant details (resume, skills, qualifications).
  • Employers can register and create company profiles to post job listings.
    Job Posting and Application:
  • Employers can post job listings with specific requirements and details.
  • Job seekers can search and apply for jobs based on preferences, location, and skillsets.
    Application Management:
  • Job seekers can monitor application statuses (pending, shortlisted, rejected, etc.).
  • Employers can review applications, provide feedback, and manage candidate interactions.
    AI-Powered Recommendations:
  • AI algorithms will recommend relevant job opportunities based on job seekersâ€™ profiles and past application behavior.
    Real-Time Notifications:
  • Job seekers will receive notifications when their application status changes or when new jobs that fit their profile are posted.
  • Employers will receive notifications for new applications and candidates that meet their job criteria.
    Database & Security:
  • A robust and scalable database structure to support users' data, job listings, applications, and profiles.
  • Secure data handling to ensure user privacy and protection.
